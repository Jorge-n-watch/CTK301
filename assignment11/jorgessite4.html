THE CLASS:

CTK 301: Web design is a course offered at ISU that is meant to teach students the fundamentals of Web design through the use of coding languages such as Html and Css. By breaking down websites to their core skeletons students are able to observe what goes on behind the scenes of their most used websites and apps.You can find a full list of classes offered under the CTK umbrella at this link:
